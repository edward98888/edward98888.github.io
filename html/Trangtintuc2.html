Read All The Lyrics To Cantrell’s Debut Album ‘THANK YOU 4 THE TRAGEDY’ 23 july 2022

The introspective MC’s first official LP is the latest Genius Distro release.

Cantrell is back with his first full-length album.

Following a stint on Nas’s Mass Appeal label, the Albany, GA native has established his own Free Minds Music and partnered with Genius Distro for the release of his official debut album THANK YOU 4 THE TRAGEDY, which is out now on all music streaming platforms. On the project, the introspective MC uses his soulful sound and high-powered lyricism to examine the various heartbreaks in his life. In the end he recognizes that the pain has strengthened his resolve and ultimately made him who he is.

“THANK YOU 4 THE TRAGEDY is an album about relationships and loss, burdens and blessings, gratitude in grief,” says Cantrell. "I wanted to provide people with a soundtrack to their life experiences, and the best way I knew how was to be honest about mine and the places they happen.”

Last month, Genius Distro released the project’s first single “NO FRIENDS,” where he comes to the conclusion that though he has bonds with people in high places, his rap dreams ultimately fall on him. “Navigating let down and frustration through making ‘NO FRIENDS’ I found myself in a place of clarity and certainty,” Cantrell says. “I realized that not having a helping hand isn’t so bad; my path to success is different.” Stream the whole project now.
